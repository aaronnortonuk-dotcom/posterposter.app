Privacy Policy

Poster Poster - Last updated: 23 March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Poster Poster accesses the following data through authorised APIs: your TikTok account profile information (via user.info.basic scope); permission to publish content to your TikTok account (via video.publish scope); your Google Calendar event data (event titles, dates, times, and locations) to generate promotional posters.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to: generate promotional poster images from your calendar events; publish those posters to your connected social media accounts; no data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens are stored securely and encrypted. Calendar data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored. Generated images are temporarily stored during the posting process and then deleted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Content is only posted to platforms you have explicitly authorised.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke Poster Poster's access to your accounts at any time through your TikTok, Google, or Meta account settings. Upon revocation, all stored tokens are deleted.
